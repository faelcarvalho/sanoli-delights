The mixture of 3 nationalities: Brazil, USA and Caribbean, gave life and a particular originality to the famous Classic Flan. A perfect dessert for any occasion. Soft, fluffy and delicious. That's what everyone who tastes our Classic Flan says!

Our Story

Saloni Delights started 20 years ago, through a woman who had a deli and liked to make puddings and share with friends. There the first flans were made. The mixture of 3 nationalities: Brazil, USA and Caribbean gave life and a particular originality to the famous Classic Flan. The encouragement of friends was essential to get the project off the ground and bring this wonder to you in the form of a dessert.
